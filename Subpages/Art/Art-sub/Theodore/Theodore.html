Rhys' Notes: Darth Theodore Duration:1 day

This was one of the cutest projects I've ever worked on. This project began as a birthday gift for a friend,but as time went on it found its way never reaching her. I learned a few things from this project, such as how large eyes make a cute character. I also discovered that hair is a pain to deal with; it gets everywhere (I learnt this by having long hair but hehe) Thankfully, the new viewport denoiser made things faster.

While rendering and testing i was wondering what absurd thing i can add to it so it becomes my own and not a generic cute pic so i was like darth vader... Heck Yeah, halo... Obviously

And i give you Darth Theodore ???
